Open First at Collabora!


I am Linux Core Technologies Lead, Client Relationship Manager and Partner at Collabora, a well respected Open Source Software consultancy that helps top companies in the world to create the next generation of technology with Open Source Software inside. Along with Sjoerd Simons we lead the Core team inside Collabora, that supports clients on hardware enablement, kernel development, base platform and generic userspace needs. I also co-manage with Tomeu Vizoso, the relationship with one the most interesting Collabora clients.


Co-founder of linuxdev-br conference

After many years traveling around the globe attending and speaking at Open Source and Linux conferences I felt we did not have any forum in Brazil to talk about upstream development of technologies from the Linux world. In 2017, together two of my best friends, Bruno Dilly and João Moreira we co-founded the Linux Developer Conference Brazil. linuxdev-br is its short name. linuxdev-br is a conference born to be international and act as a meeting point for Brazilian and international community. See our mission.

Member of the Linux community

I spent a decade as developer and maintainer in the Linux Kernel community working first in the Bluetooth subsystem as developer and co-maintainer and later moved on the graphics and media area, where I developed for drm and media subsystem where we did the Atomic Modesetting API and added support to Explicit Synchronization to mainline Linux Kernel. That helped unifying display drivers for Android, ChromeOS and Linux generic stacks.
